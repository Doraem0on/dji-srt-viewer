DJI SRT Flight Data Viewer

Visualize the SRT flight data from your DJI drone (Mavic Pro, Mavic 2 [Pro/Zoom], Matrice, Phantom [3/4/Pro]).

You can also load other file formats (GPX, KML...) and convert them to other software: After Effects (mgJSON), Virb Edit (GPX), Google Earth (KML), Excel (CSV). Save your visualizations as photos and videos.

Instructions

Turn on Video Captions in your camera settings within the DJI Go4 App and you will find SRT files alongside your movie clips. You can use this tool to interpret those files.

The data included in some drones (Mavic Pro) is not as accurate as the drone could produce, but since that is all we have we decided to apply smoothing to it to and produce a more meaningful interpretation of your flights. This is not necessarily more accurate.

You can tweak smoothness, load satellite or maps of the location, drag over the graphs or timeline to view data in specific moments/places, export your data to other formats or launch Google Maps exactly where you shot something.

Phantom 4 Pro (P4P) files have embedded subtitles instead of SRT files. You can extract the SRT data with this script.

You can also load GPS data in other formats, such as GPX, KML, JSON and GeoJSON. This is experimental, so let me know if your files do not display correctly. To load GoPro Metadata, you can extract it first using the GoPro Telemetry Extractor.

Note that so far this has mostly been tested with Mavic Pro SRT files. Bugs are to be expected.

Please get in touch here if you tried your drone SRT files and got no results. You can also suggest new features or share with us what you created with this tool.
